What is CPApacket?

CPApacket is a private internal reporting tool built by Shapeshift for use exclusively within the Shapeshift organization. It connects to QuickBooks Online to generate internal financial reports and summaries for the Shapeshift team.

CPApacket is not a publicly distributed application. It is not available on any marketplace, app store, or for use by third parties.

Data Access and Usage

CPApacket accesses the following QuickBooks Online data for internal reporting purposes:

  • Company financial summary data (profit & loss, balance sheet)
  • Invoice and expense records
  • Account and vendor lists

All data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used solely for generating internal reports within the Shapeshift organization. Data is never shared with, sold to, or made accessible to any third party.

Authentication

CPApacket uses Intuit's standard OAuth 2.0 authorization flow to connect to QuickBooks Online. Access tokens are stored securely and used only to make authorized API requests on behalf of the connected Shapeshift QuickBooks company.

You can disconnect CPApacket from your QuickBooks Online account at any time. See the Disconnect page for instructions.
