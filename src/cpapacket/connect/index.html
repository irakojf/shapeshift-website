Connect/Reconnect QuickBooks Online

cpapacket is a private internal reporting tool used by Shapeshift to generate internal financial reporting from QuickBooks Online data. This page describes the Connect/Reconnect flow for the QuickBooks Online integration.

Connect/Reconnect URL: https://apps.shapeshift.so/cpapacket/connect

How OAuth Connect/Reconnect Works

  1. Authorization is initiated from the cpapacket CLI.
  2. cpapacket starts an OAuth 2.0 flow and opens an Intuit authorization URL.
  3. The authorized user signs in to QuickBooks Online and approves access.
  4. cpapacket exchanges the OAuth authorization code for access credentials.
  5. Reconnect uses the same URL and flow shown on this page.

QuickBooks Data Accessed

The integration reads accounting data required for internal financial reporting, including company information, chart of accounts, transactions, and standard accounting reports (such as Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, and General Ledger).

Purpose

Data is used only to generate internal CPA reporting packets for accounting review and tax preparation. Data is not sold. For privacy details, see our Privacy Policy for full details.
