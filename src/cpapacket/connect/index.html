Connect Your QuickBooks Online Account

CPApacket is Shapeshift's private internal reporting tool that integrates with QuickBooks Online. This page describes how to connect your QuickBooks Online company to CPApacket.

How to Connect

  1. Log in to your QuickBooks Online account.
  2. Navigate to Apps → My Apps and locate CPApacket.
  3. Click Connect and authorize the requested permissions.
  4. Once authorized, CPApacket will securely access your financial data for internal reporting.

Reconnecting

If your connection is interrupted or your token expires, return to this page and repeat the steps above. The reconnect process uses the same URL and flow.

What Data Is Accessed

CPApacket accesses only the financial data necessary for internal reporting, including accounts, transactions, and company information. No data is shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
