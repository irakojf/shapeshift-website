Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

CPApacket is a private internal reporting tool developed by Shapeshift that integrates with QuickBooks Online. This privacy policy describes how CPApacket collects, uses, and protects data accessed through the QuickBooks Online API.

CPApacket is not distributed publicly and is not available on any marketplace. It is used exclusively by authorized Shapeshift personnel for internal financial reporting.

Data We Access

When connected to your QuickBooks Online account, CPApacket may access:

  • Company information (name, address, fiscal year)
  • Chart of accounts and account balances
  • Transaction data (invoices, expenses, journal entries)
  • Vendor and customer names

CPApacket accesses only the data necessary for generating internal financial reports.

How We Use Your Data

  • Generate internal financial reports for Shapeshift
  • Reconcile accounts and track financial performance
  • Support internal auditing and compliance processes

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties. Data is used solely for Shapeshift's internal business purposes.

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Access tokens are stored securely and refreshed as needed.
  • Access is restricted to authorized Shapeshift personnel only.
  • We do not store raw QuickBooks data beyond what is needed for active reports.

Data Retention and Deletion

When you disconnect CPApacket from your QuickBooks Online account, we immediately stop accessing your data. Previously generated reports may be retained for internal record-keeping. To request deletion of any retained data, contact support@shapeshift.so.

Your Rights

  • You may disconnect CPApacket at any time via your QuickBooks Online account settings.
  • You may request information about what data we have accessed.
  • You may request deletion of retained data by contacting support.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to connected users. The latest version is always available at this URL.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at support@shapeshift.so.
