Disconnect from CPApacket

You can revoke CPApacket's access to your QuickBooks Online data at any time by following these steps.

How to Disconnect

  1. Log in to your QuickBooks Online account.
  2. Navigate to Apps → My Apps.
  3. Find CPApacket in the list of connected apps.
  4. Click Disconnect to revoke access.

What Happens After Disconnecting

  • CPApacket will immediately lose access to your QuickBooks Online data.
  • No further data will be retrieved from your account.
  • Previously generated internal reports may still exist within Shapeshift's systems.
  • You can reconnect at any time by visiting the Connect page.

Need Help?

If you have trouble disconnecting or have questions about your data, contact us at support@shapeshift.so.
